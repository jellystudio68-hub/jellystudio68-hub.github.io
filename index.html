About Hela

Hela is designed for short gaming sessions with simple rules and fast-paced gameplay. No account or registration is required. Featuring a clean interface and smooth animations, it’s perfect for relaxing, passing time, or friendly challenges.

  • Lightweight, fast startup, offline playable
  • Multiple modes: Bomb Flip, Number Guess, Poker Dragon
  • Supports Chinese interface, continuously updated

Last updated: 2025-09-24

Main Features

For all ages

Bomb Flip

Explore the grid carefully and avoid stepping on bombs. Supports various board sizes and custom challenge levels.

Number Guess

A classic guessing game: follow hints to gradually reach the answer. Simple, fun, and great for quick play.

Poker Dragon

Decide if your card is higher, lower, or in between two revealed cards. A fast-paced mix of luck and judgment.
