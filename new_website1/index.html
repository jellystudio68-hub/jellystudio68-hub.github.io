Hela

About Hela

Hela is designed for short gaming sessions with simple rules and fast-paced gameplay. No account or registration is required. Featuring a clean interface and smooth animations, it’s perfect for relaxing, passing time, or friendly challenges.

  • Lightweight, fast startup, offline playable
  • Multiple modes: Bomb Flip, Number Guess, Goalkeeper
  • Continuously updated with improvements

Last updated: 2025-09-24

Main Features

  • Bomb Flip: Explore the grid carefully and avoid bombs. Customizable board sizes and challenge levels.
  • Number Guess: Classic guessing game: follow hints to gradually reach the answer. Simple and fun.
  • Goalkeeper: Decide if your card is higher, lower, or in between two revealed cards. Fast-paced and exciting.
